Who We Are

Founded in January 1997, ICON Engineering, Inc. is a locally owned and operated consulting civil engineering firm based in Centennial, Colorado. ICON specializes in complex floodplain modeling and hydrologic/hydraulic analyses, development of river and drainage master plans, stream stabilization and restoration improvements, design of utility and storm water infrastructure, roadway design, and geospatial solutions.
